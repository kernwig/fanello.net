Absolute Minimum Hardware Requirements for Centipede Lite

  • Commodore 128 or Commodore 128D Computer
  • 80 Column Monitor
  • At least 250 KB (1000 blocks) of disk space on a single, and preferably fast, storage unit
  • At least another 200 KB (800 blocks) of total disk space on any C128 compatible storage unit(s)
  • 1200 to 33.6 Modem (with Hayes 'AT' compatible command set)
  • If needed by your modem, a RS-232 User Port Modem Interface, a SwiftLink, or a Turbo 232 cartridge
A file transfer area (U/D) will require additional storage space - as will games if you upgrade to the full version of Centipede.
