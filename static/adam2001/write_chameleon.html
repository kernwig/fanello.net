The Rhetorical Chameleon

This written for a friend and dedicated to all those who have, or have ever had, an identity crisis.
(It's a message poem - not at all of "propper" form.)

A chameleon wanders from place to place, pretending to be what it is not.

Its true self is masked, by the world soon forgot.

What does a chameleon actually look like?

Nobody knows.

For a chameleon has
No look of its own.

And so the chameleon must ask itself,
And the chameleon must learn...

"Behind this mask of environment,
really,
Who am I truely?"
