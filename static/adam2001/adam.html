I'm Adam Fanello. Born on November 14, 1973, I cried. Hey! I was a baby. That's what babies do. As I grew up, I looked upon the world around me, and cried some more. Just kidding. Okay, if you want some real serious philosophical stuff, get inside my mind and read some of my writings. (Just don't touch anything. I'm delicate.) Actually, I do like it here in this world. I think I'll stay a while...


Here's the dry stuff:

  • Earned Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from California State University, Fullerton (CSUF) in May 1998.

  • I'm a Software Development Engineer (AKA: Computer programmer). Here's my resume.

  • I achieved some limited commerical success starting in high school, developing software for the Commodore 128 personal computer. Check out what I did; visit my Bugsoft web site.

  • Worked at the big aerospace company Raytheon - currently on a project known as WAAS (Wide Area Augmentation System). Think of it as the next generation GPS (Global Positioning Satellite) system. Then I got bored and jumped to a tiny startup called Centor.
